About Me – Priyanshu Parth

I'm Priyanshu Parth, a passionate 2nd-year B.Tech student in Electronics and Communication Engineering with a specialization in Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, currently studying at MIT World Peace University, Pune. Originally from Bhagalpur, Bihar, I've always been driven by a deep curiosity for how technology can solve real-world problems and improve lives.

I enjoy diving into the world of programming and development, with technical skills in HTML CSS Java JavaScript C C++ Arduino and web development. Whether it's working on a temperature-based fan controller, building a JARVIS-style assistant, or creating a smart home security spider robot — I love building projects that combine creativity with functionality.

Beyond my technical side, I find peace and inspiration in nature. I love nature photography, sketching, photo editing, and spending time exploring the outdoors — moments that recharge my creativity and calm my mind.

I consider myself a team player with strong leadership and problem-solving abilities. My soft skills include adaptability attention to detail critical thinking emotional intelligence and a hardworking attitude — all of which help me grow both academically and personally.

I may still be shaping my exact path, but I'm driven by a strong desire to become an expert in my field, learn continuously, and contribute meaningfully to the world of technology.
